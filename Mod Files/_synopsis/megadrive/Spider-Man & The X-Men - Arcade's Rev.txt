Spider-Man and the X-Men in Arcade's Revenge is an action game starring both the Spider-Man and X-Men comic book franchises. It was released for both the Sega Mega Drive and Sega Game Gear. The game's box art and initial title screen incorrectly label the game as Spider-Man X-Men.
