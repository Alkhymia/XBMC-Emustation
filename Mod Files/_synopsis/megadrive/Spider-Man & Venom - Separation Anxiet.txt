Venom - Spider-Man: Separation Anxiety, or simply Separation Anxiety is a beat-'em-up game based on the Spider-Man universe. It was released for the Sega Mega Drive in 1995 and is a sequel to Spider-Man and Venom: Maximum Carnage.
