The Great Circus Mystery Starring Mickey &amp; Minnie known as Mickey to Minnie Magical Adventure 2 in Japan, is a 1994 Sega Mega Drive and SNES game by Capcom in their Disney's Magical Quest series, following the 1992 SNES game The Magical Quest Starring Mickey Mouse.
