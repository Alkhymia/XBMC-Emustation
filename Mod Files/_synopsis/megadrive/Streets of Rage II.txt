Streets of Rage 2, known as Bare Knuckle II: Shitou e no Requiem in Japan, is a side-scrolling beat-'em-up for the Sega Mega Drive, released in 1993 as a sequel to Streets of Rage. It received 8-bit ports for the Sega Master System and Sega Game Gear, as well as being included on numerous compilations of Mega Drive games. More recently, it was made available on the Virtual Console and Xbox Live Arcade download services.
