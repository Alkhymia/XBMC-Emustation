Adventures of Batman &amp; Robin, The
Platform: Sega Genesis / Mega Drive

Genre: Action

Release Year: 1995

_________________________
The Adventures of Batman &amp; Robin for the Sega Mega Drive is a beat-'em-up game inspired by the cartoon series of the same name. Despite having an identical name it is completely different to the Sega Game Gear and Sega Mega CD versions.
