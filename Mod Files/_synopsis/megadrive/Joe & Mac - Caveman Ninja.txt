Joe &amp; Mac, known as Joe &amp; Mac: Caveman Ninja in Europe and Joe &amp; Mac: Caveman Fight in Japan, is a 1991 platforming action game by Data East, initially released for a DE-manufactured arcade board, but later ported to home consoles such as the Sega Mega Drive (ported by Takara).
