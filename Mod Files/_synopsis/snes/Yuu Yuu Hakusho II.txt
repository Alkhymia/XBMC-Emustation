Yuu Yuu Hakusho 2 is a 2D Fighting game, published by Namco, which was released in Japan in 1994.

Tips:

Hidden Characters
At the main screen press Down, Up, L, L, R, R, Y, X, A, Y.

Alternate Credits Screen
Beat Story Mode without losing any match. At the credits screen, you'll see images of the main characters in the background.
