Yuu Yuu Hakusho Final is a 2D Fighting game, published by Namco, which was released in Japan in 1995.

Trivia:

Unlocking Yakumo
At the title screen, press Y seven times, X six times and A five times, you'll hear a voice that the code is confirmed. He is available in Versus, Training and Tournament modes.
