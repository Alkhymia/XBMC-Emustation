If Wrestlers Had Tanks, They'd Use Them. In World Destruction League Thunder Tanks, they do. To win the WDL title, these insane tank warriors compete in battle events televised from wasted landmarks around the world. Join hosts Kate Maxwell and Chuck (Mad Dog) Clemens as they bring you all the demented play-by-play. Now that's entertainment!
