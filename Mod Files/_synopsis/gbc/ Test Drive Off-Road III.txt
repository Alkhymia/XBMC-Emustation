No roads, no rules! The world's most powerful off-road vehicles in the world's most incredible environments! Ten licensed vehicles with upgrades, including the mighty Hummer. 24 tracks in 8 exotic real world locations. Feel every bump and impact with the built-in rumble feature! Collect cars and store your victory trophies in the Test Drive custom garage. Battery back-up feature allows you to save everything without passwords.
