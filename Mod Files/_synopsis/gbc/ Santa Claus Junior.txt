Santa Claus Junior

Santa Claus Junior is a platform game for Gameboy Color. It features a little boy called Nick whose job it is to find all presents Santa Claus lost when the evil witch caught him, so of course he must defeat the witch and free Santa Claus so that Christmas can commence as usual.
