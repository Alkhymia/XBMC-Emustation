Bubby and Bobby travel to Rainbow Islands in search of hidden treasure. Instead, they find only trouble. Seven worlds, plus a special bonus world. 32 action-packed rainbow-jumping levels of play. Play a demo round to get ready for the real thing. Defeat seven unsavory enemies to earn seven colored diamonds.
