Looney Tunes - Sheep Raider
Platform: Playstation 1
Region: USA
Media: Disc
Controller: Analog Control Pad
Genre: Action - Third Person - Puzzle
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 2001
Developer: Infogrames Europe SA
Publisher: Infogrames Europe SA
Players: 1
_________________________
Help a starving Ralph Wolf steal sheep from Sam Sheep Dog. With an arsenal of Acme equipment at his disposal, Ralph is sure to concoct the perfect plan for keeping his stomach filled, or not!
The game takes place across 15 stages, each with their own requirements and challenges, so the longevity of the game is notable.
