Lay the Smackdown...again!. This follow up to the popular WWF Smackdown! features over 50 wrestlers, an improved Season mode, a bigger and better create a wrestler mode and new modes like create a taunt and create a stable (a group of wrestlers). New matches include TLC (Tables, Ladders and Chairs) and more!.
