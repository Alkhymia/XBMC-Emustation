Who is Mort the Chicken? Well, I really don't know and the chances are that neither does your kid. For those of you that are in the same boat, Mort is the hero of a game developed by AndNow for Crave Entertainment that is intended for a younger gaming audience. The game's story revolves around Mort, a chicken with super powers, who must save baby chicks that have been kidnapped by mysterious creatures from another planet.
