Jumping Flash is a first-person 3D action platformer where the player must use their triple-jump and special weapons to defeat enemies.

The story goes like this: the evil Baron Aloha and his minions the Muu-Muus have stolen all of the Earth's greatest locations to use to construct his own private retreat. It's up to the player as Commander Robbit the robotic super-bunny to save the world and stop Baron Aloha.
