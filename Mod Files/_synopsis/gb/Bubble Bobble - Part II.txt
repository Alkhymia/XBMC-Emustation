Bubble Bobble: Part 2
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy
Genre: Platformer
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1993
Developer: Taito Corporation
Publisher: Natsume
_________________________
The bubble-blowing dinosaurs continue their adventure in Part 2, where you must catch then pop enemies in the bubbles from your dino over many side-scrolling levels. Special bubbles litter the arenas that unleash special attacks including fire, floods, thunderbolts and more. Only one player can play.
