WWF Superstars
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy
Genre: Sports - Wrestling
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1991
Developer: Rare Ltd.
Publisher: LJN Ltd.
_________________________
WWF Superstars is a licensed wrestling game. Player can choose between 5 WWF superstars: Ultimate Warrior, Hulk Hogan, Macho King Randy Savage, Mr. Perfect and the Million Dollar Man to take on opponents in the ring.

There are a lot of moves to choose from: body slam, suplex, clothes line and many more as well as signature moves for each of the superstars. Two players can compete against each other via the Game Boy Gamelink.
