Tintin in Tibet
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy
Region: Europe
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy
Genre: Platformer
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1994
Developer: Infogrames
Publisher: Infogrames
_________________________
A platform game starring Tin Tin. Tintin in Tibet follows on the heel of Infograme's Asterix &amp; Obelix, with the same gameplay. Although the game will prove easy for the expert gamer, there are many levels to pass, each of which features new locations, enemies, and obstacles that serve to advance the storyline.
