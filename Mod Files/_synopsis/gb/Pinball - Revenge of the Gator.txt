Pinball: Revenge of the 'Gator
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy
Genre: Pinball
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1990
Developer: HAL
Publisher: HAL
_________________________
Revenge of the 'Gator is a pinball game with an alligator motif. There is one table, and 3 bonus areas that are accessed by knocking down specific targets in the main playing area.

There are several modes of play. First, there is the standard single player game. Second, there is a two-player alternating mode. Finally, there is a linked mode for two simultaneous players.
