Gametype: Prototype

_________________________

Load side A with OPTION. At the DOS prompt, type HORIZ.MC for a small joystick-operated routine. Press RESET and type UFO.MC for some space navigation!

Turn disk over to side B and press RESET. Type LOAD MC at the first DOS prompt and RUN 3000 to see some sample screens of this unreleased game!
