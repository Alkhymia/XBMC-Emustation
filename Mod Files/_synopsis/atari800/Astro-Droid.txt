Alone you must take on the might of the Reldans' mega invasion ship.  Their target is Earth and you are the only thing in the way.  As the most destructive machine in the known galaxy you have the ability to transform into an invincible attack fighter.  Your wits and fire power are the only things that will win the day.... Destroy or be destroyed.
