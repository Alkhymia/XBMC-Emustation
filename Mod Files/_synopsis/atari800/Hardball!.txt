HardBall!
Platform: Atari 800
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge (XEG)
Controller: Joystick, Keyboard
Genre: Sports - Baseball
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1987
Developer: Accolade
Publisher: Atari
Players: 1 or 2 VS
Programmer: Dan Thompson
Musician: Ed Bogus
_________________________

Hardball is a baseball action game for one or two players. You can throw a variety of pitches from fast balls to curve balls, steal bases, select team members, and substitute players as the game is played.
