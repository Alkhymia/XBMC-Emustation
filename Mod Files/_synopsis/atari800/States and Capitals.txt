States and Capitals
Platform: Atari 800
Region: USA
Media: Tape
Controller: Keyboard
Genre: Educational
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1980
Developer: Atari
Publisher: Atari
Players: 1
Programmer: Robert C. Polaro
_________________________

States & Capitals is an educational game about the geography of the United States. The goal of the game is to identify all 50 states and their capitals. The player sees a map of the United States with one of the states outlined. The player will then have to enter the right name of it and then its capital. The game goes on until it has gone through all the 50 states. The states appear in random order.
