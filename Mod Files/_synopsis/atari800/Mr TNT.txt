Mr. TNT
Platform: Atari 800
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Joystick
Genre: Action - Arcade
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1983
Developer: HesWare
Publisher: HesWare
Players: 1 or 2 Alternating
Licensed from: Telco, Inc.
Programmer: Harley H. Puthuff Jr.
_________________________

Based on the 1983 Telco coin-op. Every bit as difficult as its arcade counterpart, but a very good port. Move around the grid removing it as you go. Watch out for the sparks moving on the grid with you.
