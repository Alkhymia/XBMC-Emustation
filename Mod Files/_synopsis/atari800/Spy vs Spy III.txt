Spy vs Spy III
Platform: Atari 800
Region: USA
Media: Disk
Controller: Joystick, Keyboard
Genre: Action
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1987
Developer: Epyx
Publisher: Epyx
Players: 1 or 2 VS
Programmer: Jim Nangano
_________________________

The third and final installment (after Spy vs. Spy and Spy vs. Spy: The Island Caper) sees the spies on an arctic island. Once again they're doing their thing - wandering round the island searching for items and setting traps to kill each other (or more frequently themselves).

Trivia:

A fourth game in the series was briefly planned, set after the third one at the location you finish this game at, but it was never completed.
