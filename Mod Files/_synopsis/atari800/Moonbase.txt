'This game was under development by another group for Atari and I somehow obtained a demo of it. It was very early in development, but it replicated the ST game on which it is based pretty well...'

Screenshots, information and software provided with kind permission of Stephen Goss.

Andrew Dickinson adds... 'It never got done because Atari were a pain to deal with so Andy got fed up and cancelled the contract. There is reference to our dealings with Atari in the high score table of Plastron...'
