Nightstrike
Platform: Atari 800
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Joystick
Genre: Shoot 'em Up
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1983
Developer: Solitare Group
Publisher: TG Software
Players: 1 or 2 Alternating
Programmer: A. Y. Kobayashi
_________________________

Plays a little like Imagic's Atlantis. You are a tank at the bottom and shoot planes, bombers and kamikazees from above. Nice graphic effects but not a very entertaining title.
