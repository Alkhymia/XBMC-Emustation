Dawn Raider
Platform: Atari 800
Region: United Kingdom
Media: Tape
Controller: Joystick
Genre: Shoot 'em Up - Horizontal
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1988
Developer: Atlantis Software
Publisher: Atlantis Software
Players: 1
Programmer: Geoffrey Storey
_________________________

Fly your heavily armed helicopter gun-ship into the fortified complex of the super criminal that is holding the world to ransom with his nuclear armed rockets.  Guide your gun-ship through the giant underground cavern, shooting and bombing the rockets, guns and other internal defence systems to save the destruction of many of the world's major cities.
