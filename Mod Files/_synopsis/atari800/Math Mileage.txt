Math Mileage
Platform: Atari 800
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Joystick
Genre: Educational
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1983
Developer: K-Byte
Publisher: CBS Software
Players: 1 to 4 alternating
Programmer: Marcia M. Burrows
_________________________

Kids game where you steer your car left and right (you can't crash) through slalom-gates showing various numbers. The idea is to get to the target number shown at the bottom of the screen before time runs out.
