Robbo
Platform: Atari 800
Region: Poland
Media: Tape
Controller: Joystick, Keyboard
Genre: Strategy - Puzzle
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1989
Developer: LK Avalon
Publisher: LK Avalon
Players: 1
Programmer: Janusz Pelc
_________________________

In this puzzle game, you have to guide a robot through many levels involving such hazards as lasers and enemies. This game features smooth scrolling playfields and plenty of different things to do - from blowing up bombs to pushing crates around.

Trivia:

A demo version (containing the first 4 levels) was aired by Polish Rozgłośnia Harcerska radio in Radiokomputer broadcast, 1991. One could record the signal on a cassette and then load it using Atari XL/XE.
