Firebird
Platform: Atari 800
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Joystick
Genre: Action
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 1981
Developer: Gebelli Software
Publisher: Gebelli Software
Players: 1
_________________________

You are Piggo the firefighter and your job is to put out the fires caused by the Firebird. You control the left and right movement of a ladder along the front of a building and the up and down movement of Piggo on his ladder. You spray water in the windows of the building to put out the random fires popping up everywhere.
