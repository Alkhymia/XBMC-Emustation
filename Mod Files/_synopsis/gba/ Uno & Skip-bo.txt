Uno &amp; Skip-Bo
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy Advance
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy Advance
Genre: Board Game
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 2006
Developer: Black Lantern Studios
Publisher: Jack of All Games
_________________________
Be the first player or be on the first team to score 500 points in this classic family game! Play your cards by matching color, number or word with the top card of the discard pile. When you have one card left, it's Uno! Skip-Bo combines skill, strategy, and fun in a game that the whole family can play together! Be the first to play all of the cards in numerical sequence from your stockpile to win.
