Rescue Heroes: Billy Blazes!
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy Advance
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy Advance
Genre: Action
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 2003
Developer: WayForward
Publisher: VU Games
_________________________
A lightning bolt has ignited out-of-control fires everywhere! Take control of Billy Blazes on an adventure fighting fires in the forest, village and city. Billy Blazes runs, jumps, swings and dodges hazards as he quenches fires and rescues stranded victims.

The game features multiple gadgets including Billy's Fire Hose, Axe, Ladder, Grappling Hook and Sliding Hook. Time Trial modes and vehicle levels add to the variety.
