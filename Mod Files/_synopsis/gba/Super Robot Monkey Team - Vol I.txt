Super Robot Monkey Team Volume 1 lets you watch episodes from Super Robot Monkey Team on your Game Boy Advance. In "Depths of Fear," you can find out if Chiro can overcome his fear of water to save his monkey friends from an underwater monster. In "Plantetoid Q," you'll follow Chiro and the Hyperforce team through the insides of a living planet.
