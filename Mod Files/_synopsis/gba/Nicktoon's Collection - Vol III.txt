SpongeBob SquarePants, Danny Phantom, and The Fairly OddParents... All three shows are now available on the Game Boy Advance. The third volume of the Nicktoons Collection features three episodes from your favorite animated shows, including "Danny Phantom: Attack of the Killer Garage Sale," "The Fairly OddParents: Tim Visible," and "SpongeBob SquarePants: The Squeaky Boots."
