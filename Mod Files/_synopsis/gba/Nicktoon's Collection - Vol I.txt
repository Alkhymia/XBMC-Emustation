This Game Boy Advance Video pack lets you watch episodes from the All Grown Up!, SpongeBob SquarePants, and The Fairly OddParents animated series. Now, wherever you go, you can watch your favorite episodes, including "All Grown Up: Chuckie's in Love," "SpongeBob SquarePants: Pizza Delivery," and "The Fairly OddParents: The Big Problem." Join Chuckie as he tries to impress a girl, follow SpongeBob in his first pizza delivery run, and watch Timmy transform into a short, bald man.
