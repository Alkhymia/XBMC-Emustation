Tringo
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy Advance
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy Advance
Genre: Puzzle
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 2006
Developer: Crave
Publisher: Crave
_________________________
Points are earned by fitting random shapes together, each cluster of 4, 6 or 9 squares gets cleared, with more points being scored for putting together more contiguous squares. You've got to move quickly but carefully because missed pieces lose points!
