Yu Yu Hakusho: Ghostfiles - Tournament Tactics
Platform: Nintendo Game Boy Advance
Region: USA
Media: Cartridge
Controller: Gameboy Advance
Genre: Strategy
Gametype: Licensed
Release Year: 2004
Developer: Sensory Sweep
Publisher: Atari
_________________________
The game follows the Dark Tournament storyline of the popular Yu Yu Hakusho anime series brought stateside via FUNimation Productions. Gameplay is done ala 'tactics' style, similar to Tactics Ogre or Fin Fantasy Tactics. The player will assume control of Yusuke at the beginning, and will be able to add to Team Urameshi as the game progresses. Expect some surprise guests on the way to fight Toguro.
